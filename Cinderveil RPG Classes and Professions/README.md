# Cinderveil RPG Classes and Professions

A comprehensive class and profession system for Valheim that adds RPG-style character progression through Almanac custom status effects. Featuring 18 classes and 10 professions!

---

## ‚ö†Ô∏è IMPORTANT: Check your Almanac config options, or this mod won't work!

**Status Effects = On**  
**Max Effects = 2**

(You can set max effects to whatever number you want, but I recommend 2, so you can have 1 profession and 1 class. If you want to multi-class etc., set it higher, just be aware it might make your game too easy.)

---

## üìñ Overview

Adds classes with unique strengths and weaknesses, as well as professions that reward you for completing milestones. Choose from **18 classes** (8 melee, 5 ranged, 5 magic) and **10 professions**. 

This mod is meant to be used with mods that increase difficulty, such as [CLLC](https://thunderstore.io/c/valheim/p/Smoothbrain/CreatureLevelAndLootControl/), [RtD](https://thunderstore.io/c/valheim/p/Soloredis/), etc.

## ‚ú® Features

### üó°Ô∏è Melee Classes

- **Barbarian** - A savage melee fighter that relies on aggression to survive. Hits hard and fast, but lacks defense.
- **Knight** - A heavily armored melee combatant with a defensive playstyle. Slow, excels at outlasting enemies through sheer endurance.
- **Warrior** - A versatile frontliner, balanced between offense and defense. Adaptable to any weapon, they maintain steady damage in combat.
- **Monk** - A fast melee DPS specializing in fist weapons. Gains bonus movement speed, dodging, and reduced stamina costs on attacks.
- **Paladin** - A resilient melee tank that prefers a mace and shield. High health and regeneration make them hard to kill.
- **Striker** - A fast melee damage class with reduced stamina costs. Agile and relentless, but vulnerable to counterattacks.
- **Assasin** - A stealthy melee specialist. Skilled with knives and gains bonuses to sneaking.
- **Revenant** - A lifesteal-focused melee fighter who sustains through combat. Restores health while damaging, rewarding aggressive playstyles.


### üèπ Ranged Classes

- **Archer** - Masters of the bow, combing speed and precision for sustained pressure in combat.
- **Ranger** - A stealthy hunter who uses the bow to strike unseen. Gains bonuses to stealth.
- **Stalker** - A quiet ranged bow user with lifesteal.
- **Sharpshooter** - A crossbow expert who deals high-precision damage at long range.
- **Bloodbane** - A crossbow-focused ranged DPS who self-heals while dealing damage. Thrives in prolonged fights.


### üî• Magic Classes
- **Conjurer** - A mage that focuses on summoning and shielding. Takes increased physical damage, but reduced fire, frost, and lightning.
- **Druid** - A magic user with balanced skills and bonuses to health regeneration. Highly resistant to poison, but weak against blunt, slash, and pierce.
- **Mage** - Elementalist with multiple elemental damage bonuses and resistances. Vulnerable to physical attacks.
- **Warcaster** - A battlemage who balances magical ability and defense. Gains a little Eitr, health, and armor, while losing movement.
- **Warlock** - A versatile elemental caster who converts damage into vitality. Benefits from resistance to elemental damage, but reduced physical protection.

### üõ†Ô∏è Professions

- **Acrobat** - Jump Skill (+8 per level)
- **Angler** - Fishing Skill (+8 per level)
- **Chef** - Cooking Skill (+8 per level)
- **Climber** - Reduced Fall Damage (-8% per level)
- **Craftsman** - Crafting Skill (+8 per level)
- **Farmer** - Farming Skill (+8 per level)
- **Jockey** - Ride Skill (+8 per level)
- **Miner** - Pickaxes Skill (+8 per level)
- **Porter** - Increased Carry Weight (+80 per level)
- **Swimmer** - Swim Skill (+8 per level)
- **Woodcutter** - Wood Cutting Skill (+8 per level)

### üèÜ Achievement System

Unlock achievements by completing various tasks! Cook, craft, farm, fish, gather, mine, kill, and more to progressively unlock your classes and professions! (View exact requirements in-game.)

## üéÆ How to Use

1. Open Almanac in-game (press tab, then click the Almanac icon on the right)
2. Click the Achivements tab at the top.
3. Each class has 7 progressive levels with increasing bonuses. Professions have 4 levels. Complete the requirements to unlock the class/profession.
4. Once unlocked, you can select a class and/or profession to activate.

üí°TIP: Using the search box at the bottom, you can type in "profession", "class", "level 1", etc. to filter the list.

## ‚öñÔ∏è Balance Notes

- Classes provide combat-focused bonuses
- Professions offer utility and skill improvements for non-combat activities
- This mod is balanced around challenging gameplay and works best with difficulty mods
- The default recommendation of 2 max effects encourages focused builds (1 class + 1 profession) but you can set it to whatever you prefer! (If you want to be a Barbarian-Mage-Warrior-Assasin menace, I won't stop you!)
- As it currently stands, it is not possible to add negative modifiers to resistances with Almanac. So, class weaknesses to Fire, Poison, Blunt, Pierce, etc. will not work at present. Sorry about that! I hope this becomes possible in the future, for better balancing.
